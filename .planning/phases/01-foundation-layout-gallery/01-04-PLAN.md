|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|

| T-01-04 | Privacy | Instagram embed (player) | low | accept | Instagram's embed URL sets cookies regardless of embed method. No nocookie equivalent exists. This is standard behavior for Instagram embeds and is expected by users. |
