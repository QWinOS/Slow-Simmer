<objective>
Close the two Phase 1 theme gaps that block requirement UI-03 ("clean, elegant design fitting supper club brand"). Both are small, localized fixes across two files: `app/layout.tsx` and `app/globals.css`.

Gap 1 — Brand typography not applied: Karla is imported but never used (Inter is bound to `--font-sans` instead), and `@theme inline` maps `--font-heading: var(--font-sans)`, so every heading resolves to the body font. Fix so headings render Playfair Display SC and body renders Karla.

Gap 2 — Warm red/gold palette not applied: the shadcn tokens in `:root`/`.dark` still hold the default zinc/neutral OKLCH values; only a decorative unused `--brand-primary` was added. Fix so the full UI-SPEC warm hex palette drives the surface.

Purpose: These two truths are the two pillars of UI-03. Without them the phase goal ("clean, elegant supper club brand") is unmet even though the layout, gallery, and video features are structurally complete.

Output:
- `app/layout.tsx` binds Karla to `--font-sans` (replacing Inter) and keeps Playfair Display SC bound to `--font-heading`
- `app/globals.css` `@theme inline` maps `--font-heading` to the Playfair CSS variable (not `var(--font-sans)`)
- `app/globals.css` `:root` and `.dark` token blocks overridden with the UI-SPEC warm hex palette
</objective>
